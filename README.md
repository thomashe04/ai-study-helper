# AI Study Assistant

## Summary
This project is about an AI-based study assistant that helps students learn more efficiently by generating summaries and quizzes. Building AI course project.

## Background
Students often struggle with large amounts of study material.

Problems:
* Too much information
* Lack of structure
* Low motivation

* ## How is it used?
Students upload their notes and the AI creates summaries and quiz questions.

Users:
* Students
* Pupils

* ## Data sources and AI methods
Data:
* Study materials
* Textbooks

AI methods:
* Natural Language Processing (NLP)
* Machine Learning

* ## Challenges
* Data privacy issues
* AI may give wrong answers

* ## What next?
* Mobile app development
* Integration with universities
